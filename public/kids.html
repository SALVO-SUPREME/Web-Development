How to Help Animals Close to Extinction?

There are loads of animals going extinct, that means they are slowly passing away.
We don't want to live in a world where our beautiful animals pass away, and we're sure you don't want to either.
So we're going to give you advice on helping to prevent this.
A lot of extinction is caused by climate issues; so we're going to teach you ways to help prevent climate change:

  1. Turn off the lights when they're not needed.
  2. Instead of having your parents drive you, walk or cycle! (It's healthier too!)
  3. Take short showers when you can to save electricty/heating.
