Our History

We established Wilson Zoo as rescue for endangered species and animals with tormented pasts.
It is our core belief that us humans should acknowledge and respect Nature's gift to us, animals. We especially make it our mission to take in animals whom have been rescued, because we believe they deserve the right to peace and safety.
We wish to offer everybody the oppertunity to gape, gawk and gaze at our wide variety of animals, so we humbly invite YOU to Wilson Zoo.

Conserving Animals

Although we are already an organisation established on rescuing animals and giving them a safe haven, we believe it is our duty to do more.
Thats why we donate all our profits to numerous organizations throughout the world in order to aid the ferocious battle against animal abuse, endangerment and extinction.
