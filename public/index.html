Our Values

It is our core belief that us humans should acknowledge and respect Nature's gift to us, animals. We especially make it our mission to take in animals whom have been rescued, because we believe they deserve the right to peace and safety.
We wish to offer everybody the oppertunity to gape, gawk and gaze at our wide variety of animals, so we humbly invite YOU to Wilson Zoo.

What Can You Do?

Our Zoo hosts a diverse range of services, activities and events to satisfy any and everybody.
We host events which focuses on a particular animal for a week and during this week, we; refresh our cafe with meals and drinks inspired by the region the animal belongs to, share cuddly toys of the animal of the week, and implement activities for you to engage with the animal.

Our Animals

Our Zoo is a haven to a plathera of exotic animals.
We have domains dedicated solely to specific breeds, so you can traverse and acknowledge each animal's beauty and might.
