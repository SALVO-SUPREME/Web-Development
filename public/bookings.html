Events

Here at Wilson Zoo, we frequently host events to spice up your experience, we ensure that every week is unique and bursting with fun.
We host events which focuses on a particular animal for a week and during this week, we; refresh our cafe with meals and drinks inspired by the region the animal belongs to, share cuddly toys of the animal of the week, and implement activities for you to engage with the animal.

Donate To Us

Your donation will help us significantly, and not in just one way;
It will help us run our organisation by ensuring our animals here are looked after well.
It will be donated to numerous organizations throughout the world in order to aid the ferocious battle against animal abuse, endangerment and extinction.
Or you can sponsor an animal of your choice to ensure that they will survive despite their climate, no matter where in the world they are.
